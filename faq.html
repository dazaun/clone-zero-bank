Frequently Asked Questions

  1. How can I edit my profile?
  2. How can I review my transaction history?
1

How can I edit my profile?

  1. From any page, click your user name which appears at the top right corner of the site.
  2. From the dropdown menu that displays, click My Profile.
  3. Edit your profile.

2

How can I review my transaction history?

  1. Click Account Activity.
  2. Click the Show Transactions tab to view your most recent transactions.
  3. Click the Find Transactions tab to show transactions by a date range.
